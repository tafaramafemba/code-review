Elon Musk

He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; early-stage investor, CEO, and Product Architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; and co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI.

  • 1999: co-founded the web software company Zip2 with his brother Kimbal
  • 1999: co-founded online bank X.com
  • 2002: co-founded SpaceX
  • 2004: joined electric vehicle manufacturer Tesla Motors
  • 2006: helped create Solar City
  • 2015: co-founded OpenAI
  • 2016: he co-founded Neuralink
